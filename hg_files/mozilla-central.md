(Trivial reason: only ARM64 is affected   and we have no ARM64 products.  Deeper reason: stacks are aligned at   coarser boundaries than our stack chunks, and have lots of headroom.)  - Member variables tracking the stack height are now set up in a new   method, onFixedStackAllocated(), which is called after   beginFunction() has allocated the space for the Header and Local   areas of the frame.
